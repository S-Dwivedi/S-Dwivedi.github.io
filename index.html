I am a Junior Professor (W1) in the Department of Mathematics, University of Hamburg where I am a member of the Analysis and Differential Geometry group.


Before coming to Hamburg, I was a Research Associate (Postdoc) at the Institut für Mathematik at Humboldt Universität zu Berlin, where I worked with Thomas Walpuski. I got my PhD at the University of Waterloo, working with Spiro Karigiannis, my Masters at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur and Bachelors from the Banaras Hindu University.

I am mainly interested in differential geometry and geometric analysis, more specifically in various aspects of manifolds with special holonomy.
